Kalpana acts in a play

Ghosts Act 1 by Henrik Ibsen

REGINA.

[Looking hard at him.]

What are you going to fool Pastor Mander into doing, this time?
ENGSTRAND.
Sh! sh! Are you crazy? Do _I_ want to fool Pastor Manders? Oh no! Pastor Manders has been far too good a friend to me for that. But I just wanted to say, you know--that I mean to be off home again to-night.
REGINA.
The sooner the better, say I.
ENGSTRAND.
Yes, but I want you with me, Regina.
REGINA.

[Open-mouthed.]

You want me--? What are you talking about?
ENGSTRAND.
I want you to come home with me, I say.
REGINA.

[Scornfully.]

Never in this world shall you get me home with you.
